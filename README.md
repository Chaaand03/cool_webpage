# My_website

Welcome to my portfolio website! This website serves as a showcase of my skills, projects, and professional experience. It is designed to help recruiters and hiring managers learn more about me and my work.

## Features

- **About Me**: A brief introduction to who I am, my background, and my career goals.
- **Projects**: A collection of my key projects, highlighting my technical skills and problem-solving abilities.
- **Skills**: A list of my technical and soft skills relevant to the industry.
- **Resume**: A downloadable version of my resume for quick reference.
- **Contact**: Easy ways to get in touch with me, including email and LinkedIn.

## Purpose

The primary goal of this website is to assist in my job search by providing a professional and visually appealing platform to showcase my work and achievements. It is tailored to impress recruiters and hiring managers by demonstrating my expertise and passion for my field.

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website!
